A family tradition since 1987

Nonna Maria opened Bella Vista in 1987 with a simple mission: bring the flavors of her Sicilian village to New York. Three generations later, we still use her recipes, her olive oil, and her passion for hospitality.

Every dish tells a story. Every meal is a celebration. Welcome to our family table.
